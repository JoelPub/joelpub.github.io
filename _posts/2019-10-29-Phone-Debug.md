**利用Android Studio自带的模拟器联网  **    
* [Mac OS X下Android系统无法连接问题之解决方案](https://www.jianshu.com/p/8c19fb78a680)   

```javascript
system_profiler SPUSBDataType
Vendor ID："0x2717"
echo "0x2717" > ~/.android/adb_usb.ini
adb kill-server
adb start-server
adb devices
```
